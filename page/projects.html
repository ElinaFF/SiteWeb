Projects

Environmental Monitoring

Understand how the disruption of ecosystems effects microorganisms in Arctic soils using the EcoChip, a microbial in situ culture system for measuring the growth of microorganisms in their natural environment.

Breast Cancer

Deciphering breast cancer HER2-negativity with regard to HER2-targeted therapy using machine learning.

Protection of water sources

Advanced study of unconventional oil behaviour, innovative emergency measures to surface water oil spills: protection of water sources.

Drug discovery

Computational and machine learning approaches to improve design and screening of high bioactivity peptides for drug discovery.

Resistance to antibiotics

Selectomics to monitor and predict the emergence of resistance to antibiotics.

Evaluation of blood products

Evaluation of blood products in health and disease using machine learning and high throughput mass spectrometry.

Quality control of blood products

Improved quality control of blood products using high throughput mass spectrometry.

Indications against paramixoviruses

Characterization of cellular signatures of viral infections to identify compounds with new antiviral indications against paramixoviruses.

Legionella

Legionella pneumophila (Lp) is a water-borne pathogen and most LD outbreaks are due to the contamination of water-cooling towers, which aerosolize the bacterium and enable its transmission to the human lungs.

Extracellular mitochondria

Role of extracellular mitochondria in adverse transfusion reactions.

Human and Microbial Integrative Genomics

Advances in human and microbial genomics are already playing a profound role in medicine. The development of new bioinformatics tools is needed for the integration of data across multiple omics.
